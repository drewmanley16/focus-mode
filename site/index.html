macOS app

Deep Focus

One button. Total focus. Silences notifications, pauses your music, and keeps you locked in.

Download for Mac

macOS · Apple Silicon · 208 MB

How to install

1

Open the downloaded .dmg and drag Deep Focus into Applications

2

On first launch, macOS will warn it can't verify the app. Click Done

3

Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down, and click Open Anyway

4

Deep Focus appears in your menu bar. You're ready

What it does

Timed Sessions

5 to 120 minute presets. A smooth SVG progress ring counts down every second.

Silences Notifications

Enables macOS Do Not Disturb automatically. Re-enables when you're done.

Pauses Music

Stops Apple Music and Spotify so you can drop into silence immediately.

Ambient Soundscape

Warm generative audio — soft drones and filtered noise. No files, all synthesized.

Lives in Your Menu Bar

Tiny icon in the top-right. One click to start. No dock clutter.

Audio-Reactive Visuals

Subtle organic blobs in the background pulse with the ambient audio.
