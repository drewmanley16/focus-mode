Deep Focus

Fullscreen focus sessions, Pomodoro cycles, custom timers, and a clean ritual to lock in quickly.

Download for Mac

macOS · Apple Silicon · 208 MB

How to install

1

Open the downloaded .dmg and drag Deep Focus into Applications

2

On first launch, macOS will warn it can't verify the app. Click Done

3

Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down, and click Open Anyway

4

Deep Focus appears in your menu bar. You're ready

What it does

Focus Sessions

Preset durations with a minimalist ring timer and distraction-free fullscreen mode.

Pomodoro Mode

Run focus and break cycles with quick presets like 25/5, 50/10, and 52/17.

Custom Timers

Create your own named timers, including optional break durations, and launch them from the app or menu bar.

Deep Start Ritual

Optional countdown before each session to breathe, reset, and enter focus intentionally.

Menu Bar + Shortcuts

Start, stop, and skip breaks from the menu bar or with keyboard shortcuts for fast control.

Simple Settings

Toggle Pomodoro, toggle Deep Start, adjust countdown duration, and manage custom timers.
